Aryabhat Astronomy Quiz 2020-21

Online Declaration of Result for Preliminary Examination

The result was declared in the auspicious hands of Dr. Erika Hamden on September 26, 2020 at 8:30 PM IST.

Dr. Erika Hamden is an eminent astrophysicist with a focus on Ultraviolet (UV) Detection, Spectroscopy, and Galaxy Evolution. She is an Assistant Professor of Astrophysics at the University of Arizona, US, and a 2019 TED fellow.

Aryabhat Astronomy Quiz is in its 24th successful year today. In the current year's Quiz, the preliminary test was conducted online for a total of 3927 students from 248 schools from across the state of Madhya Pradesh, India.
